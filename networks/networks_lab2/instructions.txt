Server side:
python3 generator.py
Takes input bitstring on the first line and 
two space separated fractions on next line

Ex.
$ python3 generator.py
1010100001001
0.5 0.7


Receiver side:
After the inputs are given to the generator,
Sound would start to play
Then run 
python3 recording.py

Ex.
$ python3 recording.py
